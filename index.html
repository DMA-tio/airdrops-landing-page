FAQs

What is a crypto airdrop?


A cryptocurrency airdrop is a free giveaway of crypto tokens by a blockchain project or ICO to members of a certain crypto community (usually holders of other crypto tokens).

Participants of airdrops receive free tokens straight into their wallets.

What is the trade.io airdrop campaign?


The trade.io airdorp program allows holders of TIO (the trade.io token) and registrants of the trade.io community to earn free tokens from ICOs that are part of our ICO Consulting & Advisory services.

How do I get free crypto tokens?


You can earn free tokens through the trade.io airdrop program by holding TIO (maximum benefit), by joining the trade.io community on Telegram, or by participating in our listed ICOs.

What are the rules for the airdrops and how can I qualify?


We list only strictly-vetted ICOs that are deemed by us to either have high potential as an investment or to significantly contribute towards the project's respective industry or blockchain technology.

Why do ICOs give out freek tokens?


Blockchain projects and ICOs distribute free tokens to the crypto community in order to raise awareness of and increase demand for their tokens, and as part of partnerships with other blockchain projects like trade.io.

Which ICOs and projects are listed for airdrops?
